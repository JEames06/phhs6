## Measurements

- Labour productivity
- Output per employee
-  Staff Turnover

### Employee retention

- All businesses loose staff
	- Natural causes (illness, death)
- Changes in strategy
	- Closing a branch
- Bad management or conditions
	- If people are unhappy with the conditions of the job they are more likely to leave
