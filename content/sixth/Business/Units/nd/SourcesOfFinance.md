## Potential Sources

### Long-term

- Finances the whole business over many years

- Share capital
- Retained profits
- Venture capital
- Mortgages
- Long-term bank loans


### Medium-term

- Finances major projects or assets with a long life

- Bank loans
- Leasing
- Hire purchase
- Government grants

### Short-term

- Finance day to day trading of the business

- Bank overdraft
- Trade creditors
- Short-term bank loans
- Factoring
